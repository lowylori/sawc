ArcGIS Online Methodology

Log into ArcGIS Solutions, through the ArcGIS Online portal. Search for the relevant solution you are looking for; in this case it was “Parks and Grounds Management”. Click the “Deploy now” button to deploy this solution so your account.

Once deployed on your account, you are able to start editing the components you would like to use. Firstly, open both surveys in Survey123 Connect to be able to edit them. While doing this, make sure to update the titles and names on the surveys to match the current project. Once any edits are made to the survey to meet the requirements you are looking to meet, republish the survey.

Next, edit the Workforce project to add any relevant staff to the project and assign them roles. These staff members will now be able to see any open requests assigned to them.

Next, add data to the surveys to ensure the surveys are being presented the way you are anticipating and so you have data to test the dashboards and workforce with.

Log back into Workforce and practice assigning an open task to a staff member. This is to test out that the task assigns properly and that this change is reflected in the dashboreds explored in step 6.

Next, edit the dashboards and requests centers to match clients name and needs. By selecting and editing each layer separately, update the titles and names of the dashboards to match the project. On the dashboard, ensure the data being presented makes sense in relation to the project goals and select new questions to be presented in the graph area if needed.
