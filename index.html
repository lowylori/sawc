Southern African Wildlife College: Security, Heath and Safety, Environment and Quality Solution

Problem Statement

Ideal

A centralized platform where anybody can report a problem on campus and then it be assigned to a specific person/department depending on the zone and tracked using a dashboard. Generate automated reports to summarize problems reported and derive trends.

Reality

Right now they collect their data and track it all separately, and they do much of it by paper. There is no efficient way to report results, there are many different places they have to look for data. There is no way to track productivity and how efficiently tasks are getting done.

Consequences

They will not have a centralized way to track issues and progress on their issues and things may fall through the cracks. This causes a certain level of risk for the residents on campus in terms of security, heath and safety, environment and quality.

Proposal

Create a centralized platform that allows the college to assign and track progress for SHEQ (security, health and safety, environment, and quality) tasks categorized by pre-designated zones of their campus and level of priority. Solution should be easy to use and require minimal or no maintenance.

Technology Used

ArcGIS online Ecosystem (ArcGIS Workforce, FieldMaps, Survey123, ArcGIS Dashboards), Power Automate, Excel onedrive
